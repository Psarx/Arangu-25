FISAT CUP

FISAT Cup is an annual sports event organized by Federal Institute of Science And Technology (FISAT). The event aims to promote physical fitness, sportsmanship, and team spirit among the students of the institute.

The FISAT Cup encompasses a range of indoor and outdoor games, including but not limited to football, cricket, volleyball, badminton and so on. The event is usually held for a week, and students from all the departments participate in it. But this time, here it is to indulge you in a thickly packed two day competitive enthusiasm.

The FISAT Cup is more than just a sporting event; it is an opportunity for students to bond with their peers and create cherished memories. The institute encourages students to participate in sports and provides them with the necessary facilities to pursue their interests. Overall, its a celebration of sports, culture, and camaraderie that reflects the values of FISAT.
