Split Screen

An on-stage dance event where two performers showcase their skills while separated by a barrier. Without the ability to see each other, the challenge is to maintain rhythm, synchronization, and creativity, proving that coordination can transcend visual cues.

Rules:

  • Time Allotted: Approximately 7 minutes (including setup)
  • Participants: Two performers (one on each side)
  • Performance: Must be executed without any visual communication between the two sides
  • Judging Criteria: Synchronization, creativity, musicality, and overall execution

Convotron

Let the diagrams take life. Participants in this challenge will receive a block diagram and be tasked with using their imagination and knowledge to bring the design to life. To do so, they will draw circuits and produce the corresponding outputs for the inputs. Prizes will be awarded based on the speed and accuracy of their work, making for an exciting competition that tests both technical and swiftness of the participants.

Pillow Fight

Pillow Fight is a playful, energetic event where participants engage in a friendly pillow fight to create a lively and humorous atmosphere. It encourages fun and lighthearted competition in a safe environment.

Treasure Hunt

Treasure Hunt is an exciting event where participants follow a series of clues and solve puzzles to uncover hidden treasures, testing problem-solving abilities and teamwork.

Penalty Shootout

Penalty Shootout is a competitive event where participants test their football shooting skills under pressure, aiming to score with precision and technique.

Sumo Wrestling

Sumo Wrestling is a physically challenging event where participants engage in friendly matches inspired by traditional sumo, testing strength, balance, and technique.

Triple Treat

Triple Treat is a basketball event that tests basic skills like shooting, dribbling, and passing in a fun, energetic environment, emphasizing teamwork and sportsmanship.

Arm Wrestling

Arm Wrestling is a head-to-head contest that tests strength, technique, and endurance as competitors aim to pin each other's arm using a combination of power and proper form.

Target Shoot

Target Shoot challenges participants to demonstrate precision and focus by hitting targets at varying distances, with scoring based on accuracy and speed.
